FAQs

Find answers to common questions about Levels Club Pokhara. If you don't find what you're looking for, please contact us.

We update our FAQ regularly to provide the most current information about our club policies, events, and services.

General Questions

What are your opening hours?

Levels Club is open:

  • Everyday: 9:00 PM to 6:00 AM

Special events may have different hours which will be announced on our social media.

What is the dress code?

We enforce a smart casual dress code:

  • No sportswear, flip-flops, or beachwear
  • Collared shirts preferred for men
  • Dress shoes or fashionable sneakers
  • No excessively revealing clothing

Management reserves the right to deny entry based on dress code at their discretion.

Do you have age restrictions?

Yes, we strictly enforce the following age policies:

  • Minimum age for entry: 18 years
  • Valid government-issued photo ID required for all guests
  • No exceptions will be made
  • Alcohol will only be served to guests 21+ with valid ID

Reservations & Events

How can I make a reservation?

Reservations can be made:

  • Online through our website booking system
  • By calling +977 9802853733 during business hours
  • Through direct message on our Instagram or Facebook

For large groups (8+ people) or VIP tables, we recommend booking at least 48 hours in advance.

Do you host private events?

Yes! Levels Club is available for private events:

  • Birthday parties
  • Bachelorette parties
  • Corporate events
  • Product launches
  • Special celebrations

We offer full buyout options or semi-private areas. Contact our events team at events@levelsclubpokhara.com for packages and pricing.

Is there an entry fee?

Our entry policy varies:

  • Regular nights: Free of Cost
  • Ladies night (Wednesday): No cover for women
  • Special events: Varying cover charges as announced
  • VIP table reservations include entry for your group

Facilities & Services

Do you have a coat check?

Yes, we offer secure coat check services:

  • Location: Near the main entrance
  • Fee: Free of Cost
  • Hours: Open until 30 minutes after closing
  • We cannot be responsible for valuable items left in coats
Is there parking available?

Parking options near Levels Club:

  • Street parking available but limited
  • We recommend using taxis or ride-sharing services

The club is not responsible for vehicles or items left in vehicles.

Do you serve food?

We offer a selection of bar snacks and light meals:

  • Menu available until morning
  • Vegetarian and non-vegetarian options
  • Special dietary requests accommodated when possible
  • No outside food permitted

For full dining options, we recommend visiting one of Lakeside's excellent restaurants before coming to the club.
