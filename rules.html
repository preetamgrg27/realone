Club Rules & Regulations

To ensure everyone has a safe and enjoyable experience, please follow our club rules:

Entry Requirements

  • Valid government-issued photo ID is mandatory for entry (must be 18+ years)
  • Management reserves the right to refuse entry without explanation
  • Dress code is smart casual - no sportswear, flip-flops, or beachwear
  • No outside food or beverages allowed
  • All bags are subject to security checks

Conduct Inside the Club

  • Respect all staff and fellow patrons - harassment will not be tolerated
  • No fighting or aggressive behavior will result in immediate removal
  • No smoking except in designated areas
  • No illegal substances of any kind
  • Do not leave drinks unattended
  • Photography/videography is allowed but respect others' privacy

Alcohol Policy

  • No alcohol served to intoxicated persons
  • No sharing of alcoholic beverages with minors
  • No bringing in outside alcohol

Other Important Rules

  • No weapons of any kind allowed on premises
  • No vandalism or damage to club property
  • The club is not responsible for lost or stolen items
  • Management reserves the right to eject anyone violating rules without refund
  • These rules are subject to change without notice
