LEVELS CLUB POKHARA

Premier nightlife destination in the heart of Lakeside

Our Story

Located at Centerpoint, Lakeside, Levels Club Pokhara has been the go-to destination for nightlife enthusiasts since its establishment. Our club offers an unparalleled experience with its vibrant atmosphere, state-of-the-art sound system, and breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains.

We pride ourselves on creating memorable nights where music, drinks, and good vibes flow endlessly. Whether you're looking to dance the night away or enjoy cocktails with friends, Levels Club provides the perfect setting.
